Cook everything below on Sunday in ~75 mins. Lasts the full week. Daily meals are just assembly + reheating.

Chicken tikka batch (2.2 kg)

Marinate whole tray of chicken breast in yogurt, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, salt. Bake at 200°C for 22 min. Portion into containers — Vasu gets 180-200 g portions, partner gets 130-150 g. Stays good 5 days in fridge.

45 min (mostly oven time) — enough for 5 days of lunches and dinners for both

Big pot of tadka dal

500 g red lentils, pressure cook or simmer 20 min. Tadka: ghee + cumin seeds + dried chilli + garlic + tomato + turmeric. Mix in, simmer 5 min. Split into daily portions. Vasu gets 200 g serves, partner gets 160 g. Makes ~7-8 portions total for each.

25 min — cheapest protein per gram on the list, high iron for partner

Dozen hard-boiled eggs

Boil 12 eggs for 10 min, cool, leave unpeeled in fridge. Grab and go all week. Used in breakfasts and snacks for both.

10 min

Rice + oats portioned

Cook 600 g dry basmati (makes ~1.5 kg cooked). Portion into 150 g serves for Vasu, 100 g for partner. Freeze half if needed. Dry oats need no prep — just portion into overnight jars or cook fresh (3 min microwave).

20 min

Masala base (optional but great)

Fry 3 onions, 4 tomatoes, ginger-garlic in oil until thick paste. Cool, freeze in ice cube tray. Drop 2-3 cubes into anything — dal, eggs, tuna — instant flavour, zero effort during the week.

15 min — makes about 14 portions

Weekly shop for two. ALDI first, fill gaps at Coles/Woolies.
