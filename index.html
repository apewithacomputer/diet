Sunday prep — ~75 min total
Do this once on Sunday. Everything else during the week is reheating + assembly. Verified quantities cover both people for 7 days.
1Chicken tikka batch45 min
Start with 2.3 kg raw chicken breast (covers Vasu ~2.3 kg + girl gets keema on Sunday separately). For Vasu: marinate in yogurt, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, ginger-garlic, salt. Bake 200°C for 22–25 min. Portion: 320–350 g raw portions for Vasu.

For girl: mince remaining ~1.3 kg raw chicken breast. Mix with finely grated potato, onion, green chilli, garam masala, semolina (2 tbsp), cornflour (1 tbsp), salt. Shape into patties (~180 g raw chicken per 2-patty serve). Refrigerate raw — airfry 200°C 6 min each side as needed each day.
Vasu: 7 lunch/dinner serves · Girl: 6 patty serves + 1 keema serve
2Tadka dal — big pot25 min
Rinse 1.5 kg raw red lentils. Pressure cook 8 min (or simmer 20 min) with turmeric and salt. Yields ~3.75 kg cooked dal. Tadka: ghee + cumin seeds + dried red chilli + garlic + grated tomato. Mix in and simmer 5 min. Portion into containers — Vasu 200 g cooked (~65–70 g raw equivalent), girl 160 g cooked (~52–60 g raw equivalent). Fridge 5 days, freeze the rest.
~28 portions total — covers both for full week
3Hard-boiled eggs — 18 eggs12 min
Boil 18 eggs for 10 min, ice bath, leave unpeeled in fridge. Used all week in breakfasts and snacks. Both people pull from the same batch. Make a second batch mid-week if needed.
Covers both people for Mon–Thu · Make second batch Thu evening
4Basmati rice batch20 min
Cook 700 g raw basmati — rinse until clear, 1:1.5 water ratio, 15 min covered. Yields ~1.9 kg cooked. Vasu portions: 55 g raw = ~150 g cooked. Girl portions: 37 g raw = ~100 g cooked. Freeze half in zip-lock portions — rice reheats perfectly in 90 sec microwave.
~18 combined serves
5Masala base (freeze)15 min
Fry 4 large onions + 5 tomatoes + 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste in 2 tbsp oil until very thick and dark. Cool, pour into ice cube tray (~1 tbsp cubes). Freeze solid, then transfer to a zip-lock. Drop 2–3 cubes into anything during the week — eggs, tuna, dal, patties — instant deep flavour with zero effort.
~20 cubes · lasts 4 weeks in freezer

Vitamin D3 — 1000–2000 IU daily
Ostelin D3 or any ALDI/Coles generic D3. Take with a fatty meal (lunch or dinner) — D3 is fat-soluble. One tablet daily. That's it — diet covers everything else. Don't need a B-complex, zinc, or magnesium unless symptoms appear.

Partner
Ferro-Grad C — 1 tablet daily
Ferrous sulfate 325 mg + sodium ascorbate 562 mg. Take on empty stomach first thing in the morning. No tea or coffee for 1 hour after. Dark stools are normal. Full ferritin recovery takes 4–6 months. Recheck iron studies at 3 months.
Priority — take daily without fail
Partner
B-Complex (methylfolate + B6 + B12)
Homocysteine at 17.8 μmol/L won't come down from food alone at this level. Look for: Blackmores Executive B, or any supplement containing methylfolate (not just folic acid), B12 ≥500 μg, B6 ≥10 mg. One daily with food. ~$15 for 60 tabs at Coles/Woolies. Retest homocysteine in 3 months.
Non-optional — homocysteine 17.8 needs treatment
